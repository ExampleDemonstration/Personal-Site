Bio

I am a former aircraft mechanic with over 11 years experience in both heavy & line maintenance. I decided to make a career change post-COVID due to the unpredictable long hours, travelling, and overall lack of work-life balance. I have always been passionate about tech, & have a creative, curious, technical, analytical & methodical mindset. I am open-minded, attentive to detail, with a strong problem-solving ability. These personality traits along with the notion that programs, apps, & tech can solve real-world problems & contribute to making peoples lives easier, exciting & more productive are what excite me & made me want to pursue a career in coding.
